Objective of the Game:

Obtain a combination of cards with a value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.

Beat the dealer by achieving a better combination or waiting for the dealer to exceed 21.

Start Game:

Click "Start Game" to begin a new game with two random cards for both the player and the dealer.

Show New Card Value:

Click the "Show New Card Value" button to generate a random card value for the player or the dealer.

Restart Game:

Click "Restart Game" to reset the game, clear the cards, and prepare for a new round.
